Some info about me

Since childhood, I’ve been fascinated by how computers work and respond to human interactions.
That curiosity led me into computer science — starting from IT helpdesk, moving into programming, and eventually into QA.

I currently work at the HPS Center of Excellence in Greece, focusing on the credit product of the PowerCARD system. During my time at HPS, I’ve also contributed to key projects such as the AlphaBank migration to PowerCARD, the 3DS implementation for the National Bank of Canada and other banks, as well as the initial launch of Cosmote Payzy.

Apart from my main role as QA, I’m also actively involved as a BA and lead, mentoring junior and mid-level testers and coordinating small QA teams.
